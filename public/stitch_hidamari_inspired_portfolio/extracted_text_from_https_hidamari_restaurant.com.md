## Manila, capital of the Philippines   
Japanese restaurant in the center of Makati City

リトル東京やマカティスクエアから徒歩３分ほどに位置するクリークサイド４階のひだまりレストラン。 

ひだまりという店名は、忙しい毎日を過ごす中で、ほっとする優しい空間、実家に帰った時のような暖かい雰囲気のようなお店になればなと思い、心を込めて名付けました。

Hidamari Restaurant is located on the 4th floor of Creekside, just a 3-minute walk from Little Tokyo and Makati Square.

I chose the name Hidamari with great care, as I wanted it to be a comforting space where people can relax amidst their busy lives, and to create a warm atmosphere reminiscent of returning to one's parents' home.

## Hidamari SPACE

最大65名までご利用いただける、  
広くて開放感のある店内。 

お昼は光が差し込み明るい雰囲気、  
夜は暖かいライトのある店内でお食事。 

お酒も生ビール(サッポロプレミアム生、黒生、ハーフアンドハーフ、キリン一番搾り生)、焼酎、ウイスキー、日本酒、ワイン、シャンパン、カクテル、フレッシュジュースなど多数あり、 ボトルーキープも可能です。

お席はカウンター、ダイニング、個室２つ（8名〜10名様・16名様〜18名様まで対応）の３タイプ。 

活気のある暖かいスタッフ、日本語が話せるスタッフ、ひとりで来ても、誰と来ても過ごしやすい。それが当店の魅力です。 

Our restaurant offers a spacious and   
open space that can accommodate  
up to 65 people.

During the day, you can enjoy your meal in a   
bright and vibrant atmosphere, while at night,  
the restaurant creates a cozy ambiance with warm lights.

We also have an extensive selection of alcoholic beverages, including Japanese draft beer(Sapporo Premium Draft, Kuro Draft, Half and Half, Kirin Ichiban Shibori Draft), Shochu, Whiskey, Japanese sake, Wine, champagne, and cocktails, and fresh juices.

Furthermore, we offer bottle keeping services for your convenience.

There are three types of seating options available: Bar counter, dining area, and two private rooms.

Our friendly and attentive staff, who are fluent in Japanese, create a lively and welcoming environment, making it easy for you to enjoy your time at our restaurant, whether you're dining alone or with others. That's the charm of our establishment.
